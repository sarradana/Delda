ABOUT

I'm Delneshin Danaei, majored in information science and interested in photography and design. Although, my field of study seems far from my interests, it was where I started my first design work by designing ILISA newsletter cover and layout. Actually, it wasn't my first experiment in art; I had taken two great years of photography classes with Shahriar Tavakoli in mahe-mehr institute before. Later, I attended Tahereh Taban & Farhad Fozuni graphic classes on Vijeh School of visual art.

I see a sense of humor in every part of life and I try to add a bit of humor to my works. So, not surprisingly Martin Parr and Peyman Hushmandzadeh are among my favorite artists. I believe that inspirations are all around us. So, we just need to look around, search for things, observe and add some creativity to them. This way, we are learning everyday just by living in this world!

Currently, I'm getting my Ph.D. in Information Science, and I'd like to research on how people interact with art in their everyday life and the impact of art & aesthetics on people's attitude.

Beside photography and design, I play the Santoor (traditional Iranian instrument), take care of Kakoli (my cockatiel) and share my love life with Hamid (my husband).

I'm a freelancer living in Tehran, a city of big paradoxes!

Every words from you would be a pleasure to me, please feel free to contact me.
